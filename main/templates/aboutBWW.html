Personal

Ben is the oldest of 5. Born in Fargo ND, he spent most of his childhood in Edina MN. As a senior in High School, Ben moved away from home to play in the Western Hockey League (Victoria, BC), where he earned tryouts with the Minnesota Wild and Winnipeg Jets, as well as, earn the Fan Favorite and Unsung Hero Awards. After the 2013-14 season he signed on with the St.John's IceCaps in the AHL (WPG Jets Affliate). He then relocated with the team to Winnipeg where he'd split time with the Manitoba Moose (AHL) and Tulsa Oilers (ECHL). Following that season he moved to Ceske Budejovice (Czech Republic) to again play professionally. He then ended his hockey career the following season due to injury in Friburg, Germany. During the course of these 8 years Ben traveled to 38 states, 7 provideneces, and 11 countries. The experiences and knowledge of cultures and traditions not only helped him to better understand the world, but also helped him to understand that wherever you go, no matter what you do, people are just people. We are all equal but we may live entirely different realities based on upbringing, our communal standards, and traditions. These differences he believes should be celebrated, and that those who are free and in a season of blessings, should use their season to help those who are struggling. Whether it's helping those abroad or those who are homeless on the very streets we walk. It's our duty to care and help.
